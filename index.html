Hello!
My name is Christian Denis and I am a Canadian physicist and artist. I am currently living in the wonderful city of Montréal and am studying Honours Physics at McGill University.

In physics, I am primarily interested in theoretical reasearch on dynamical systems. I am very fond of extremely hands-on arts and crafts, as well as fundamental abstract ideas about math and physics. I believe this has to do with the artistic quality these two seemingly opposite branches have in common.

I have also worked on multiple musical projects, such as studio albums and short film scores. I am always open to collaborate and contribute to various artistic projects.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website!
