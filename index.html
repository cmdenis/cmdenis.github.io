Hello!
My name is Christian Denis and I am a Canadian physicist and artist. I am currently living in the wonderful city of Montréal and am working as a research assistant with prof. Paul François at the University of Montréal.

In physics, I am primarily interested in theoretical research on dynamical systems and computational sciences. Most of my work has been in the field of biophysics. More specifically, I have worked on numerical simulations and the characterization of nonlinear oscillators (in the context of somitogenesis).

I have also worked on multiple musical projects, such as studio albums and short film scores. I am always open to collaborate and contribute to various artistic projects.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website!
